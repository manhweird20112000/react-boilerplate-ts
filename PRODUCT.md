# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal admin and operations users maintaining business data in an app-style interface. They work with dense tables, filters, pagination, and create/edit flows, often moving quickly across many records.

## Product Purpose

Provide a reliable internal tool to view, create, update, and manage operational data with minimal friction. Success means fast scanning, predictable interactions, and confident correctness under time pressure.

## Brand Personality

Dense, power-user oriented, and professional. Prioritize clarity, efficiency, and “always know where you are” structure over decoration.

## Anti-references

- Bootstrap-like UI (heavy borders, coarse spacing, dated feel).

## Design Principles

- Make dense information legible through hierarchy, not extra chrome.
- Optimize for repeat workflows and speed, without sacrificing correctness.
- States must be unambiguous: loading, disabled, focus, error, and empty.
- Consistency wins: once a pattern exists, reuse it everywhere.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus-visible, keyboard navigation, and reduced motion support where applicable.
